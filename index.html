The practice

A boutique advisory built for real and recurrent change.

Turner Adaptive Strategies focuses on the kind of work that reaches beyond strategy slides and into the realities of how organizations actually operate.

Two decades inside complex, regulated organizations taught me that the difference between institutions that move and ones that stall is rarely strategy. It's whether knowledge is visible, legible, and ready before the next disruption forces the question.

I'm engaged when leadership intent has outpaced operational reality, when the knowledge that should guide decisions is stuck in bylaws no one sees, in data no one queries, or in the experience of leaders no one has properly onboarded.

The work is senior and hands-on. What you get is strategic clarity, operational discipline, and the fortitude to design change that holds.

Services

Four signature engagements,
and the work that follows.

i.

Knowledge Audit

Bylaws · Playbooks · Continuity

A structured assessment of how your organization creates, shares, and retains knowledge across core documents, program playbooks, and team workflows. The result is a practical strategy that identifies continuity risks and closes the knowledge gaps that matter most.

ii.

Data Discovery Workshop

Impact · Donor Landscape · Market Funnel

An intensive workshop focused on the systems already collecting your most valuable information, including program impact, donor behavior, and market signals. You’ll leave with a clearer understanding of what your data can answer today and where the gaps still are.

iii.

Culture Audit

Collaboration · Unspoken Rules · Volunteer Retention

An honest assessment of how the organization actually operates, including collaboration patterns, unwritten norms, and the conditions that keep volunteers and staff engaged. The goal is to identify where culture supports the mission and where it may be holding the organization back.

iv.

Executive Onboarding & Coaching

Context · Public Conflict · Mission to Ops

Personalized development for incoming executives and directors: building real contextual fluency, navigating conflict in the public eye, and translating mission and vision into operational decisions that hold up under scrutiny.

v.

Advisory Engagements

Strategy · Process · Change · AI Readiness

For organizations facing challenges that don’t fit neatly into a single category, these engagements provide ongoing strategic support across operations, process design, leadership, and AI readiness.

The approach

Three commitments.
Every engagement.

i.

Clarity before motion

Design · Document · Deploy
